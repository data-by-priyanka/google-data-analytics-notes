# Building a Professional Online Presence

A strong online presence can:

- Help potential employers find you
- Connect you with other data professionals
- Allow you to share your work and insights
- Keep you informed about industry trends
- Help you participate in community events

Your online presence represents your professional identity.

---

## LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform designed to connect people within the same industry.

It helps you:

- Build professional relationships
- Follow industry trends
- Learn from experienced professionals
- Share career updates and achievements
- Engage with the data analytics community

Maintaining a complete and updated LinkedIn profile increases visibility and credibility.

---

## GitHub

GitHub is both a code-sharing platform and a professional collaboration space.

It allows you to:

- Share projects and portfolios
- Collaborate with others
- Contribute to open-source work
- Document your learning journey
- Manage team projects

A well-organized GitHub profile demonstrates practical skills and consistency.

---

# Networking

Networking is professional relationship building.

It allows you to:

- Connect with other analysts
- Join industry groups
- Learn from peers
- Discover opportunities
- Exchange knowledge and insights

Strong professional relationships can support long-term career growth.

---

## Mentor vs Sponsor

### Mentor

A mentor:
- Shares knowledge and experience
- Provides guidance
- Helps you develop skills
- Supports learning and growth

A mentor helps you improve.

---

### Sponsor

A sponsor:
- Advocates for your advancement
- Recommends you for opportunities
- Supports your career progression within an organization

A sponsor helps you advance.

---

## Key Takeaway

Technical skills are essential.  
But visibility, relationships, and community engagement accelerate career growth.

A strong professional presence builds credibility and opportunity.
